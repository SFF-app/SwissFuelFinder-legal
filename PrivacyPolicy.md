# Swiss Fuel Finder - Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 2024

Your privacy is paramount. This policy explains how Swiss Fuel Finder (SFF) handles your data in compliance with the **Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP)** and the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**.

## 1. Data Controller
**Company/Owner Name:** [Your Name / Company Name]
**Email:** info@swissfuelfinder.com

## 2. Information We Collect
- **Identity & Contact:** When you sign in via Google, we receive your email address and a unique ID to manage your account.
- **Location Data:** SFF uses your device's real-time GPS location to calculate distances to fuel stations.
    - *Note:* Precise location data is processed locally and is NOT stored permanently on our servers.
- **User Profile:** Your chosen pseudo, vehicle tank size, average consumption, and favorite stations are stored in encrypted Google Firestore databases to sync across your devices.

## 3. Purpose of Processing
We process your data to:
- Provide and improve the core functionality of the app.
- Personalize your fuel savings calculations.
- Authenticate community contributions (price updates).
- Manage premium subscriptions.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We use the following trusted providers:
- **Google Firebase:** For authentication and database hosting.
- **Google AdMob:** To display advertisements to free users. Google may use advertising identifiers to serve personalized ads.
- **OSRM (OpenStreetMap):** To calculate real-world driving trajectories.

## 5. GDPR Rights
Under GDPR, you have the following rights:
- **Access & Portability:** Request a copy of your stored settings and favorites.
- **Rectification:** Update your vehicle profile at any time in the settings.
- **Erasure:** Delete your account and all associated data by contacting us or using the in-app deletion request.
- **Withdraw Consent:** Revoke location permissions at the system level at any time.

## 6. Data Security
We use industry-standard HTTPS encryption for all data transfers. Your data is stored on secure Google Cloud servers located in Europe.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be announced via an in-app notification.

If you have questions about your data, please reach out to **info@swissfuelfinder.com**.
